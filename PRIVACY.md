# Privacy Policy

**Pablo Offline AI LLC**

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Summary

Pablo Enterprise and Pablo Briefing are privacy-first applications. All AI processing occurs locally on your hardware. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data.

## What Data We Collect

**None.** We do not collect any data from you.

## Local Data Storage

The following data is stored ONLY on your local devices:

### Pablo Enterprise (Desktop)
- Conversation history (stored in your chosen data folder)
- AI model files (downloaded to your machine)
- News source preferences
- Smart home device configurations
- Application settings

### Pablo Briefing (Android)
- Server connection settings (IP address, port)
- Display preferences
- Cached news articles (for offline viewing)

## Network Communication

### Pablo Enterprise
- Downloads AI models from HuggingFace (one-time, on your request)
- Downloads backend files from GitHub (one-time, on your request)
- Fetches RSS feeds from news sources you configure
- Fetches weather data from weather APIs
- Communicates with smart home devices on your local network
- **No data is sent to Pablo Offline AI or any third party**

### Pablo Briefing
- Connects ONLY to your personal Pablo Enterprise server
- Uses Tailscale VPN (optional) for secure remote access
- **No data is sent to Pablo Offline AI or any third party**

## Third-Party Services

- **Tailscale** (optional): If used, Tailscale's privacy policy applies to VPN connectivity
- **RSS Feeds**: News sources you add may have their own tracking on their websites
- **Weather APIs**: Weather data requests include your configured location

We do not use:
- Analytics services
- Crash reporting services
- Advertising networks
- User tracking of any kind

## Data Security

- All data stays on your devices
- Network communication with your server uses local network or encrypted Tailscale tunnel
- AI processing happens entirely on your hardware

## Children's Privacy

Our applications do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since we collect no data from any users, this includes children.

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, there's nothing to:
- Request access to
- Request deletion of
- Export

Your local data can be deleted by uninstalling the application or deleting the data folder.

## Open Source

Pablo Enterprise uses open-source AI models and components. No proprietary cloud services are required.

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted to this page with an updated date.
