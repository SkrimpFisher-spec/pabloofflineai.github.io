How It Works

Two Components. Complete Privacy.

Pablo consists of a powerful desktop AI environment that powers a mobile companion app that connects securely over your private network. The offline desktop component can be used without the app.

Pablo Desktop

Windows

Your personal AI runtime. Pablo is an environment designed for you to run any AI model you choose—download one from the millions available online, or let our guided installer recommend and fetch the best model for your hardware automatically.

  • Auto-detects NVIDIA, AMD, or CPU
  • Bring your own model or use our recommendations
  • 2M entity knowledge base included
  • Smart home integration
  • Free to download

Pablo Android

Android

Your *optional* mobile gateway to Pablo. Chat with your AI from anywhere on your network. Get briefings, control smart home devices, and access your private AI assistant on the go.

  • Unified social feed across platforms
  • News & weather briefings
  • Connects securely via Tailscale VPN
  • Calendar & smart home controls
  • One-time purchase, no subscription

Security First

Your Data Never Leaves Your Hardware

Unlike cloud AI services, we have no servers, no databases, and no access to your conversations. Because the best way to protect your data is to never collect it.

Zero Data Collection

We don't collect telemetry, usage data, or analytics. No accounts required. No tracking. Nothing leaves your machine.

Local Processing

All AI inference runs on your hardware. Your conversations, documents, and queries never touch external servers.

No API Keys Required

You don't need OpenAI, Anthropic, or any third-party API keys. The AI runs completely independently on your system.

Works Offline

Once installed, Pablo works without internet. Perfect for air-gapped environments, travel, or simply maintaining privacy.

Open Model Ecosystem

Choose from thousands of open-source models. Llama, Mistral, Qwen, and more. You control exactly what AI runs on your system.

No Subscriptions

Pay once for the mobile app, use forever. The desktop software is completely free. No monthly fees, no usage limits, no upsells.
